Oakmoss Wood Wallets

Hold a piece of art every time you reach for your wallet. The Oakmoss Wood Wallet is a handmade carving born from passion and crafted with precision by artisan Jesse Boyes in Abbotsford, BC.

Each wallet is made from premium hardwoods like padauk, birdseye maple, walnut, and cherry.

These final 25 wallets are extra special, as I'm offering custom carved designs burned onto them.

What began with a simple client request blossomed into a beautiful accessory cherished by over 50 happy customers.

Compact and sleek, the Oakmoss Wood Wallet effortlessly fits into your front pocket or even a shirt pocket. When you're traveling light, such simplicity is a relief.

The high-quality elastic from Warkov Shafir securely holds anywhere from 1 to 18 cards, adapting to your needs without compromising style or functionality. The genuine leather cash holder tightly grips your folded cash or receipts while granting quick and easy access.
