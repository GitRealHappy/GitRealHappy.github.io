Oakmoss Luxury Wallets

Imagine holding a piece of art every time you reach for your wallet. The Oakmoss Wood Wallet isn't just an accessory—it's a handmade carving born from passion and crafted with precision by artisan Jesse Boyes in Abbotsford, BC.

Each wallet is made from premium hardwoods like padauk, birdseye maple, walnut, and cherry. This final production of 25 wallets is extra special, featuring exclusive designs carved and burned onto them. They're the culmination of a journey that began with a simple client request and blossomed into a beloved accessory cherished by over 50 happy customers.

Compact and sleek, the Oakmoss Wood Wallet effortlessly fits into your front pocket or even a shirt pocket. When you're traveling light such simplicity is a relief. The high-quality elastic from Warkov Shafir securely holds anywhere from 1 to 18 cards, adapting to your needs without compromising style or functionality. The genuine leather cash holder tightly grips your folded cash or receipts while granting quick and easy access.
