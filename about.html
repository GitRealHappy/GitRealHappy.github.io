About Oakmoss Woodcraft

Oakmoss Woodcraft is a small woodworking business based in the Pacific Northwest, specializing in custom furniture and wooden art. Our founder, Jesse James Boyes, is a self-taught woodworker who has been creating beautiful and functional pieces for over 20 years.

Jesse's work is inspired by the natural beauty of the region, and he uses sustainable and reclaimed materials to create unique and timeless pieces.
