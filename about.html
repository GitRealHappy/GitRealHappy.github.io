Jesse James Boyes

Wood Artist and Author of Taoist Threads

I'm here to help people unlock their creative power and reconnect with the natural world to live more intentional, fulfilling lives. My wood art is inspired by the deep wisdom of nature, while my writing explores philosophy, art, and spiritual insight--aiming to help others find clarity and purpose.

I also teach the basics web development in online courses to empower people with greater digital independance, and live with greater autonomy.

Alongside all of this, I guide somatic awareness work to support deep body centred healing, helping people move through trauma and reconnect with their full potential. I'm not actively seeking clients, but am on a continuous learning journey that involves being open to working with people who seek me out.

All of this is leading toward my vision of building a small educational eco-village: The Perrennial Academy will be a place of healing, permaculture foodscaping, and conscious living that inspires others to create their own transformative communities.

You can see more about all of these non-woodworking topics here: https://www.jesseboyes.com
