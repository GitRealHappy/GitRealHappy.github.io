Jesse's Interviews With Writers, Artists, and Ethnobotanists

Jesse Interviews Wade Davis: Explorer of Cultures

Wade Davis is a renowned anthropologist and ethnobotanist. His work delves deep into the traditional cultures and knowledge systems of indigenous peoples around the world.

Jesse Interviews Monica Gagliano: Plant Intelligence

Monica Gagliano explores the fascinating world of plant cognition and behavior, delving into how plants might communicate and react to their environments in ways previously thought impossible.

Jesse Interviews Trevor Carolan: Eco-Literature

Trevor Carolan reflects on the intersection of nature and literature, discussing how the natural world shapes storytelling and the role of eco-literature in environmental activism.

Jesse Interviews Dennis McKenna: Ethnopharmacology

Dennis McKenna explores the traditional use of plants in various cultures, particularly focusing on the medicinal and psychoactive properties of these plants and their cultural significance.
