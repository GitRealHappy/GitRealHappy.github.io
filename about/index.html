Jesse James Boyes

Wood Artist and Author of The Unblocked Carver

Trees are full of stories. I carve them.

I also teach The Creative Wayfinder, a course on living your creative purpose.

I study philosophy, spirituality, and psychology, and weave my wild adventure stories into my free letters on living your creative purpose, The Unblocked Carver which you can read here.

My grand vision is to one day build a small educational eco-village: The Perrennial Academy will be a place of healing, permaculture foodscaping, and conscious living that inspires others to create their own transformative communities.

You can see more about all of these non-woodworking topics here: https://www.jesseboyes.com
